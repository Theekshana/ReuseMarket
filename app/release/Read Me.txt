This application offers a platform for users to share and distribute used items.

1.Users can log into the application using their email and password, or they can sign in with their Google account.

2.In the Home activity, users can explore the complete list of available items.

3.Users have the option to search for specific items within the application.

4.Users can upload their used items along with images, either by capturing images or choosing from their gallery. 
  Additionally, they can edit or delete the details of their listed items.
